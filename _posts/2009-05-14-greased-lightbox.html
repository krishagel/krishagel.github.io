I was using a Greasemonkey script called Greased Lightbox to simplify viewing pictures from websites like Digg, or Google Search Results. Unfortunately it also had the effect of adding a bunch of worthless junk to posts that I made that included pictures. This has now been remedied, and any of the posts that had this have been cleaned up. I apologize for any inconvenience.
